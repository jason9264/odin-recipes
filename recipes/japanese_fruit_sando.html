Japanese Fruit Sando

Description

This recipe is for a delicious, fluffy, and lightly sweetened japanese sandwich.

Sure to be a hit at any parties, picnics, and perfect for any occasion"

  • Japanese Milk Bread
  • Assortment of fruits
  • Whipping cream
  • Sugar
  • Butter

Steps

  1. First prepare all fruits so that they are clean and peeled
  2. Toast and butter the milk bread.
  3. Whip cream and add sugar to desired sweetness.
  4. Apply a layer of cream on milk bread.
  5. Follow with fruit and another layer of cream.
  6. Refrigerate and chill to allow flavors to combine.
