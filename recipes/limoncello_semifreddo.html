Limoncello Semifreddo

Description

Give all of your guests a surprise with a refreshing, sweet, and decadent dessert.

Deliciously lemony, and delightfully fresh lets start this recipe!

  • Lemon, zested and juiced
  • Sugar
  • Limoncello
  • Lemon Curd
  • Cream Cheese
  • Mascarpone
  • Finger Biscuits

Steps

  1. Combine lemon zest, juice, sugar and limoncello in a bowl. In another bowl, beat lemon curd and cream cheese with electric beaters until smooth. Beat in mascarpone.
  2. Line a 1L loaf pan with plastic wrap, leaving plenty overhanging. Dip 8 sponge fingers in the limoncello mixture, then use to line the base of the pan. Spread over half of the cream mixture. Repeat, then finish with a layer of sponge fingers. Cover with plastic wrap and freeze for 6 hours or overnight until set.
  3. Meanwhile, for the lemon syrup place sugar, 100ml water and lemon zest in a pan over medium heat. Stir until the sugar dissolves, then simmer for 5-6 minutes until slightly thickened. Transfer to a bowl and chill to cool and thicken.
  4. Place the semifreddo in the fridge to soften for 30 minutes before serving, then thickly slice and serve drizzled with syrup.
